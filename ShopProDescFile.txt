 List of functionalities that ShopPro offers:

1. **Inventory Management:**
   - Add new items to the inventory, including product code, name, price, and quantity.
   - View existing inventory items with details such as product code, name, price, and available quantity.
   - Update inventory information, including prices and quantities.
   - Delete items from the inventory when necessary.

2. **Purchase Tracking:**
   - Record purchases made by customers, including product details, quantities, and purchase dates.
   - Calculate total purchase amounts for each transaction.
   - Provide customers with payment details for their purchases.

3. **Customer Management:**
   - Maintain customer records, including names and purchase history.
   - Enable customers to view their purchase history and payment details.
   - Ensure secure authentication mechanisms for customer accounts.

4. **Admin Dashboard:**
   - Access an admin dashboard for managing various aspects of the system.
   - Perform administrative tasks such as adding, updating, and deleting inventory items.
   - Monitor inventory levels and receive notifications for low-stock items.
   - Analyze sales data and generate reports for business insights.

5. **Dynamic Pricing:**
   - Apply dynamic pricing strategies such as discounts and markups to individual items or across the inventory.
   - Set pricing rules based on factors like sales volume, seasonality, or promotions.

6. **Data Visualization:**
   - Visualize inventory data and sales trends through interactive charts and graphs.
   - Gain insights into popular products, sales performance, and customer preferences.

7. **Real-Time Updates:**
   - Receive real-time updates on inventory changes, purchases, and system notifications.
   - Stay informed about critical events and take proactive measures as needed.

8. **Security Features:**
   - Implement secure authentication mechanisms to protect user accounts and sensitive data.
   - Ensure data integrity and privacy through encryption and secure storage practices.

9. **User-Friendly Interfaces:**
   - Provide intuitive interfaces for both administrators and customers, ensuring a seamless user experience.
   - Facilitate easy navigation and efficient data entry for enhanced usability.

10. **Scalability and Flexibility:**
    - Scale the system to accommodate varying mall sizes and complexities.
    - Customize features and functionalities to meet specific business requirements and preferences.
